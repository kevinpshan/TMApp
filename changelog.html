What's New

Release history for the Toastmaster Meeting App
Version 1.1
May 2026
Current
  • Word of the Day — set today's word from the Ah-Counter menu. It appears at the top of the tally grid with a gold highlight and is tracked separately from regular filler words.
  • Add filler words on the fly — tap + Add Filler Word at the bottom of the tracking grid to add a new word during a live session without leaving the screen.
  • Bold timer colors — green, yellow, and red backgrounds are now full-screen bold colors, much easier to read across the room.
  • Cancel button on timer — you can now exit the timer without saving by tapping Cancel.
  • Clear X on name input — tap the ✕ inside the name field to instantly clear what you've typed and reset the roster list.
  • Filler count merging — if the same speaker is tracked more than once in a meeting, their filler counts now add together instead of being overwritten.
  • Backup and restore — export your full roster, filler words, and meeting logs to a JSON file. Import on any device to restore your setup.
  • Manual and changelog — tap Manual in the footer for a full guide to every feature.
Version 1.0
May 2026 · Initial Release
Launch
  • Timer — full-screen color cues for Table Topics, Evaluations, Ice Breakers, and Speeches, plus a custom mode for any other role.
  • Ah-Counter — tap-to-count tally cards for AH, UH, UM, ER, SO, LIKE, and YOU KNOW.
  • Speaker roster — type a name once and it's saved permanently for future meetings.
  • Meeting report — all timed sessions and filler counts in one place, with Copy and Share.
  • Markdown export — format the report as a table for Slack and email.
  • Installable PWA — add to your home screen for a full-screen app experience with no browser chrome.
